Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cramberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with the festive focaccia appetizers topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cramberries, and creamy brie. Il will become your new favourite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoon salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oli, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cramberries
